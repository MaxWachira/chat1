About JibuBot MMUST

JibuBot MMUST is an intelligent virtual assistant designed specifically for Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology students and staff.

Our mission is to make campus life easier by providing instant, accurate answers to your university-related questions. Whether you need information about courses, schedules, campus facilities, or administrative processes, JibuBot is here to help.

The chatbot uses natural language processing to understand your questions and provides responses based on the university's official information. It's constantly learning and improving to serve you better.

JibuBot is available 24/7 through this website, ensuring you can get help whenever you need it, wherever you are.
